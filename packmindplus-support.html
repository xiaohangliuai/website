Common Issues & Solutions

App crashes when opening

If PackMind+ crashes upon launch, please try the following steps:

  1. Ensure your device's operating system is up to date
  2. Force close the app and restart it
  3. Delete and reinstall the app
  4. Check if your device meets the minimum requirements (iOS 13+ or Android 7.0+)
Sign-in problems

If you're experiencing issues with sign-in:

  1. Verify your email address and password are correct
  2. Reset your password if necessary
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Ensure you've created an account (new users need to sign up first)
In-app purchases not working

If you're having trouble with in-app purchases:

  1. Verify your payment method in your Apple ID or Google Play account
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Ensure you're signed in with the correct account
  4. If you've already purchased premium features but they're not showing, try restoring purchases from the settings menu
Reminders not firing

If you're not receiving reminder notifications:

  1. Check that notifications are enabled for PackMind+ in your device settings
  2. Verify that Do Not Disturb mode is not active when reminders should trigger
  3. Ensure the app has permission to send notifications
  4. Check that the reminder was set with the correct date, time, and recurrence pattern
